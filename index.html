About
A little about me
S·I

I'm Sifon Aniefiok Imaikop — a software engineering and cybersecurity student, writer, and builder of ideas. I believe that technology and language are two sides of the same coin: both are tools for making the invisible visible.

By day, I study systems, write code, and think about how information flows safely through the world. By night — and sometimes long past night — I write. Essays, poems, reflections, and fragments of thought that refuse to be reduced to a function or a formula.

I started this website as a home for things I create: software that solves problems, writing that asks questions, and poems that do both. I'm drawn to ideas that sit at the edge of disciplines — where technology meets philosophy, where security meets trust, where a line of code and a line of verse share the same architecture.

When I'm not at a desk, I'm probably reading, thinking about systems I don't yet understand, or looking for the right words for something I haven't yet written.
